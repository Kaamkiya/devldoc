Welcome to devl

Devl is a CLI app to teach coding, and provide resources and cheat sheets for all possible needs.

About devl

Devl was originally supposed to be a command-line app that taught beginners how to do things, and gave them quizzes that they could try. However, it evolved into something very different. It's now a CLI app that has cheat sheets, quizzes, and a ton of resources to help people find anything they might need help with while coding.
